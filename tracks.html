We invite researchers, academicians, and industry practitioners to submit their original, unpublished research to the International Conference on Intelligent Systems and Ambient Communication (ISAC 2026) – LNCS, Springer India. We welcome high-quality contributions across a broad spectrum of themes, including—but not limited to—AI, Machine Learning & Robotics, Emerging Technology Trends, Business Intelligence, Cyber Secure Systems, and Communication Systems. All accepted and presented papers will be published in the prestigious Springer proceedings, ensuring global visibility for your work.
